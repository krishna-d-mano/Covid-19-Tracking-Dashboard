WHAT IS COVID-19?

Covid-19 is a novel respiratory disease that is part of the coronavirus family. Covid-19 stands for Corona (co) virus (vi) disease (d) of 2019 (19). It was first reported in Wuhan, Hubei Provence, China, on December 31, 2019.

For More About What Covid-19 Is: CDC on 'What Covid-19 Is'

HOW DOES COVID-19 SPREAD?

Covid-19 spreads when aerosols and respiratory droplets are passed on from a person, who is infected with the virus, to another who gets the virus because of that interaction.

For More About How Covid-19 Spreads: CDC on 'How Covid-19 Spreads'

HOW TO PREVENT COVID-19?

There are many ways to prevent getting infected with Covid-19, but the most popular and effective ways are to wear a mask, stay at home, and socially distance with other people.

For More About How To Prevent Covid-19: CDC on 'How To Prevent Covid-19?'
